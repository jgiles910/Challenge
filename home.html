Welcome

Welcome to the Rothko Museum — a small, fictional museum celebrating the emotional power of color and abstraction. Founded by two passionate collectors, our space is designed to invite quiet reflection and close looking. Explore rotating exhibitions, learn about the artist, and plan your visit using the Tickets page.

Featured exhibition: Color & Light

Who was Mark Rothko?

Mark Rothko (1903–1970) was an influential painter known for large-scale color field paintings that aim to engage viewers emotionally. His work strips away figuration in favor of luminous fields of color, encouraging deep, contemplative attention.
